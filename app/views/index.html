About

The Expression Station is designed to showcase and share participants unique qualities.

Through slow motion black and white video recordings, participants are encouraged to express themesleves.

At the end of Burning Man, consented videos will be compiled into a single video showcasing the unique participants of the temporary community.

Consent

By selecting the "Consent" button, you are allowing the artist to capture and display your likeness both during and after Burning Man.

Videos will be selected and shared outside of Burning Man at the discretion of the artist.

No Consent

By selecting the "No Consent" button, you are allowing the artist to capture and display your likeness only during Burning Man.

Your video will be deleted after Burning Man ends.
