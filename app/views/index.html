About

The Expression Station (Aka: Moving Portrait Simulator) is an interactive art installation featuring the residents of Saguaro Man.

During the day, residents are encouraged to record a short slow-motion video of themselves expressing their unique qualities.

During the night, the videos are played for the entire community as inspiration and entertainment.

After Saguar Man ends, select consented videos will be compiled into a single video showcasing the unique residents of the temporary community.

Consent

By selecting the "Consent" button, you are allowing the artist to capture and display your likeness both during and after the Saguaro Man event.

No Consent

By selecting the "No Consent" button, you are allowing the artist to capture and display your likeness ony during the Saguaro Man event.

Your video will be deleted after Saguar Man ends.
